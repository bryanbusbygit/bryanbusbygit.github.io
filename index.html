Manuscripts

Transient Length Boundary for Monomial Dynamical Systems In prep
We establish upper and lower bounds on the transient length of boolean monomial dynamical systems in terms of the Frobenius number of the set of directed cycle lengths and the diameter of the associated dependency graph.
Arithmetical Structures on Cycles with a Multi-Edge In prep
Using principles underlying the Euclidean algorithm, we construct strongly smooth arithmetical structures on cycles with a multi-edge.
