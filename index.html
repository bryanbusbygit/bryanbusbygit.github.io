Home

I work in discrete dynamical systems and graph theory, with a focus on transient behavior in state graphs and related combinatorial structure. This page collects research projects, manuscripts, and a few places where I share mathematics online.

Current work includes bounds on transient lengths for monomial dynamical systems and arithmetical structures on cycles with a multi-edge.

Manuscripts

Transient Length Boundary for Monomial Dynamical Systems (with O. Colón-Reyes) In prep
Bounds on transient lengths via cycle structure in the dependency graph and the induced Frobenius problem on cycle lengths.
Arithmetical Structures on Cycles with a Multi-Edge (with A. Díaz-López) In prep
Construction and enumeration of strongly smooth arithmetical structures on cycles with a multi-edge via Euclidean-algorithm parametrizations.
