About

I am currently an undergraduate at the University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez. My interests lie in graph theory and combinatorics. I am actively seeking research opportunities to connect with other mathematicians. I enjoy creating content that makes advanced topics accessible to beginners (see updates).

Research

  • 2024 – Discrete Dynamical Systems (Algebraic Dynamics) with O. Colón-Reyes.
  • 2023 – 5G RF Propagation Measurements with GNU Radio (Debugging Hardware) with R.A. Rodríguez-Solís.
  • 2021 – Adjacency Matrices & Walks (Graph Theory) with E. Benvenutti.

Papers

Transient Length Boundary for Monomial Dynamical Systems (with O. Colón-Reyes) In prep
Derived bounds on system transients via cycle decomposition of the dependency graph and analysis of the induced Frobenius problem from cycle lengths.
Arithmetical Structures on Cycles with a Multi-Edge (with A. Díaz-López) In prep
Applied the Euclidean algorithm to generate strongly smooth arithmetical structures on cycles with a multi-edge.
